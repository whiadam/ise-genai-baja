Intro: 
I explained that this was a paper prototype of an app and asked the participants to act like they were using it.

Tasks:  
Task 1: Find where issues are happening on campus.  
Task 2: Find the most reported issues.

Notes:  
Both participants were able to complete the tasks. One hesitated when using the map, and the other paused when looking at the trending list.

Feedback:  
They said the app was easy to use but suggested making it more clear what is clickable.

Results: 
Some parts of the app were not clearly interactive. To fix this, I would make buttons and clickable areas more obvious.
