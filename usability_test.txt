Intro  
I explained that this was a paper prototype of an app and asked the participants to act like they were using a real app. I also asked them to think out loud while completing the tasks.

Tasks  
Task 1: Find where issues are happening on campus.  
Task 2: Find the most reported issues on campus.

Notes  
Participant 1 was able to complete both tasks but hesitated when using the map and was unsure if the pins were clickable.  
Participant 2 completed both tasks but paused when looking at the trending list and was unsure if the items could be clicked.

Feedback  
Participant 1 said, “I didn’t know I could click the map pins at first.”  
Participant 2 said, “I thought the trending list was just information, not something I could click.”

Results  
One issue was that users were unsure if the map pins were clickable. This may be because they were not clearly labeled. To fix this, I added a label saying “Tap pin to view details” and made the pins more noticeable.  

Another issue was that users did not know the trending list was interactive. This may be because it's on paper or just plain text. To fix this, I added a label saying “Click to view details” and made each issue look more like a button.
